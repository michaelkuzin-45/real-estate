Dr. Nina Aks, DMD

Dr. Nina Aks has been a trusted presence in the Germantown community for over a decade. She earned her Doctor of Dental Medicine degree and has dedicated her career to building long-term relationships with patients of all ages.

Her approach is simple: listen first, recommend only what's truly needed, and make every patient feel completely at ease. She is fluent in both English and Russian, making her practice a welcoming home for Germantown's diverse community.

Dr. Aks stays current with the latest techniques through continuing education and brings those advances to her patients in a calm, modern environment.
